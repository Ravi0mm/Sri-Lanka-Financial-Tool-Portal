How It Works

The calculator multiplies the gross payment amount by the withholding rate to estimate the tax to be withheld. For resident service fees that carry a monthly threshold, only the amount above the threshold is taxed.

Choosing the Correct Rate

Withholding tax rates in Sri Lanka vary by income type, payer status, and residency. Use the rate in your contract or the latest Inland Revenue Department guidance.

FAQ

What is withholding tax (WHT)?

WHT is tax deducted at source on certain payments such as interest, rent, or professional fees.

Does this calculator handle exemptions?

No. Enter 0% if the payment is exempt, or confirm the correct rate with official guidance.

Is the net payment the amount received?

Yes, it shows the gross payment minus the tax withheld at source.
