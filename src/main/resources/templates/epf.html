How It Works

The model adds annual EPF contributions equal to 20% of yearly salary (8% employee + 12% employer), then applies annual compounding for each service year.

Current Sri Lanka Tax Rules

EPF treatment and retirement withdrawals can be affected by policy updates, tax circulars, and fund administration rules. Use this output for planning and validate with current EPF guidance before action.

FAQ

Which contribution rates are used?

Employee 8% and employer 12%, totaling 20% of monthly salary.

Can I change the interest rate?

Yes. The default is 8%, but you can adjust it to model different long-term scenarios.

Does this include ETF?

No. This calculator estimates EPF only.
