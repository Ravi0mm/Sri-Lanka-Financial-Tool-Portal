How It Works

The calculator annualizes your income (monthly salary x 12 + bonus + allowances), then applies progressive slabs from 0% to 36% and computes tax per band.

Current Sri Lanka Tax Rules

This tool uses a practical 2026 progressive slab model: first LKR 1,200,000 at 0%, followed by higher rates of 6%, 12%, 18%, 24%, 30%, and 36% for higher income bands.

FAQ

Does this include non-cash benefits?

No. Only salary, annual bonus, and other cash allowances entered in the form are considered.

Why is monthly net salary an average?

Bonus and allowance totals are spread across 12 months to provide a clear monthly planning number.

Is this a legal filing tool?

No. It is an estimate tool. Always confirm filing amounts with current Inland Revenue guidance.
