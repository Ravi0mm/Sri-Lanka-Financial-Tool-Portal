FAQ

Is EMI fixed for the entire loan?

This result assumes a constant interest rate. Variable-rate facilities can change monthly EMI.

Does this include processing fees?

No. Only principal and interest are included in the output.

Can I model 0% interest?

Yes. The calculator handles 0% by dividing principal equally across months.
